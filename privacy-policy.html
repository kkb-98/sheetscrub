SheetScrub

Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 7, 2026

What data we access

SheetScrub accesses only the data in your currently open Google Sheets spreadsheet, specifically the range you select for cleaning.

What we do with your data

  • All data processing happens locally within Google's infrastructure.
  • Your spreadsheet data is NEVER transmitted to external servers.
  • Your spreadsheet data is NEVER stored outside of your Google Sheets.
  • We do not collect, store, or share your spreadsheet content.

What we store

  • Daily usage count (number of rows cleaned) — stored in your Google account's user properties. This resets daily.
  • License/subscription status — stored in your Google account's user properties.

Third-party services

  • Lemon Squeezy (for payment processing and license key management if you subscribe to Pro). Lemon Squeezy acts as the Merchant of Record. See Lemon Squeezy's privacy policy.
  • When you activate a Pro license, your license key is sent to Lemon Squeezy's API for validation. No spreadsheet data is ever sent to Lemon Squeezy.
  • No other third-party services are used.
